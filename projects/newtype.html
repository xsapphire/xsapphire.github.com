▮Introduction

The Stanford Newtype Anime club website became my personal project since my freshman year when I became unsatisfied with the old,colorless website. Over the years the website has experienced three versions of redesign and reconstruction because of both user needs and my skill improvement. While the past versions of the website are lost, here I offer several screenshots of the past designs. The website is currently not under maintenance.
