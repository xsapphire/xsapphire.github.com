EventMap
Discover fun events around you.

▮Introduction

Eventmap is a Google Map API based app that schedules and views on-campus events for Stanford students. In event map, student club leaders post information on upcoming events, while all users view real-time events around the campus on the map. This project is under implementation over this quarter as one of my independent research.

▮Problem

This project is inspired by my friend Kristy, the leader of a student arts group on campus, has been trying hard to find people to come to her club’s performance, but was deeply disappointed by the actual turnout. Most of the people she managed to invite were already her friends, so she needs to find a way to advertise her performance to the general student population. Moreover, as Stanford students are usually very busy, invited friends sometimes can’t come because of some other urgent events, or they merely forget about it. After considering other alternative platforms for distributing information, such as posters, a mailing-list, or the club’s website, I propose implementing an app based on Google Map API that schedules and views on-campus events.

▮Implement Details

  • EventMap is implemented with the Android Studio and is targeted at Android users.
  • Utilized Google API (Placepicker, Timepicker and Datepicker), and Google Firebase Realtime Database for back-end data.
  • Some known issues with this prototype are:
    1) The order of "my hosted events" should be reversed.
    2) Both "adding to a club as a member" and "remove from a club as a member" haven't been tested.
    3) The data in notification is hardcoded.
