▮Introduction

The Openproof Courseware website is run by CSLI, Stanford, and provides web-based services for instructors and students who bought their products. I joined the team as the principal designer and front-end developer over the summer internship in 2017, and modernized the UI of the website over the two months. I mainly take input from the project manager who has been working with with the customers for many years during my design. While the website is still in development, some samples are offered here.
