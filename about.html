Resume

Since I discovered my interest in human-computer interaction (HCI) in summer 2016, I have been intensively engaging myself in the app/website design processes. In these projects, I have been playing the role of principal designer. I had achieved self-fulfillment and great fun. I feel excited to join projects in various fields, meet and work with different people, and help teams with developing products. My interest, learning and working experience in HCI have a profound influence over my design attitude.

▮ Value Users' Needs
I view user needs on top of anything else in the designing process. Therefore I always conduct a careful user research in the first place to make sure that I will take the right path to deliver discernible value to my users.

▮ Propose Alternative Designs
I believe that parallel design results in better product. I usually create two to four designs at the same time. After merging my designs with my teammates', we assess the different ideas and integrate the best features from each design into on a single concept for continued development.

▮ Reasons for Every Design Choice
In the case that members on the same team have different opinions on a design choice, I usually propose to carry out a user test or consult experts who know our target users well.
